# 🏟️ The Battleground: "The Tub" (Enshinki-Jo)

In the center of the Great Pint-Vessels lies **The Tub**, a massive, cylindrical arena where the fate of texture is decided. It is the only place where the "Spin-Jitsu" can be performed at its maximum intensity.

## 🏛️ Architecture: The Cylinder of Combat
* **The Crystal Walls:** The arena is enclosed by towering, translucent walls made of **polycarbonate-diamond**. These walls are scratch-resistant but vibrate violently when the RPMs increase. Spectators watch from behind these glass layers as the battle blurs into a whirlwind.
* **The Floor (The Leveling Plane):** To a Creami-Shinobi, the floor is sacred. It is perfectly flat to prevent any mechanical imbalance.
* **The Dueling Pits:** The floor is notched with **concentrically placed rectangular pits**.
  * These pits serve as "anchors" for the combatants.
  * During a duel, the pits can retract or raise, forcing the ninjas to adjust their $P_{AC}$ levels to match the depth of the "freeze."
* **The Central Hub:** In the exact middle of the arena is a rotating pedestal where the **X-Processor Shuriken** is locked into place before a "Processing Dash."

## ⚔️ The Ritual of the Duel: "The Final Spin"
Duels in The Tub are not fought to the death, but to the **"Smoothness."**

1.  **The Drop:** The duelists are lowered into the cylindrical arena from the **Flapper Gate** above.
2.  **The Stabilization Phase:** Combatants must stay within the rectangular pits. If they step out before the "Freeze Timer" hits zero, they lose their structural integrity and become "Soupy."
3.  **The Spin:** Once the signal sounds, the entire floor begins to rotate. The ninjas must use their weapons (The Blade or The Blender) to carve through the icy obstacles generated by the Crystal Clan.

## ⛈️ Environmental Hazards: The "Icy Build-up"
The Tub is a dangerous place due to the **Centrifugal Force**:
* **Wall Cling:** As the arena spins, failed ingredients and defeated foes are flung against the crystal walls. This creates a "crust" of hard ice that the ninjas must avoid, or they will be "scraped" by the rotating blades.
* **The Bottom-Void:** If a ninja falls into the deepest concentric pit, they risk being trapped in the "Unprocessed Zone"—a dark place where the blades cannot reach, leaving them frozen forever.

## 🧪 The Science of the Arena
* **Concentric Pits:** These represent the different **Volumes** of the Ninja Creami containers (Pint vs. Deluxe). The deeper the pit, the more "torque" is required to escape.
* **The Scraper Effect:** In the comic, the "Walls" of the arena represent the outer edges of the pint where ice crystals often hide. The ninjas must "scrape the sides" to ensure the entire battlefield is smooth.
