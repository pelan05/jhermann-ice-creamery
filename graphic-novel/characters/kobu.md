## 🦹 Villain File: Kobu (The Hump)

In the world of the **Crystal Clan**, there is no greater terror than **Kobu**. While other villains use cold or snow, Kobu uses the most dangerous weapon of all: **Uneven Expansion**. He is known as the "Blade-Breaker" because he is the only enemy Kuri-0 cannot simply spin through.

### 🎨 Visual Design (Anime Style)

- **The Body:** A massive, asymmetrical giant. One side of his body is smooth, but his right shoulder and back are covered in a jagged, rock-hard **"Mountain of Ice"** (The Hump).
- **The Texture:** His skin isn't smooth; it has the rough, dull texture of **Freezer Burn**. He is surrounded by a cold, dry mist that sucks the moisture out of the air.
- **The Armor:** He wears "Unleveled Plate," armor that is purposefully lumpy and tilted.
- **Color Palette:** Dirty Grey, Icy Translucent Blue, and "Warning Red" for his eyes.

### 📊 Vital Stats (The Science of Destruction)

| Attribute | Level | Specialization |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Hardness** | MAX | Solid water ice with 0% sugar—harder than steel. |
| **Surface Tension** | Extreme | His body is never flat, making him impossible to "process." |
| **Blade Fatigue** | S-Tier | His presence causes mechanical stress to any weapon. |
| **Clan Rank** | High General | The Crystal Clan’s "Unstoppable Wall." |

### ⚔️ Combat Style: "The Axis-Wrecker"

Kobu doesn't dodge attacks. He waits for Kuri-0 to spin, and then he shifts his weight to destroy the Ninja's equipment.

1. **The Center-Point Strike:**
   - Kobu focuses all his mass into a single, raised peak. When a blade hits this "hump" at high speed, the vibration snaps the blade’s shaft instantly.
2. **Freezer-Burn Fog:**
   - He exhales a cloud of dry ice that removes all "Lube" (fats/oils) from the air, making Kuri-0’s joints grind and overheat.
3. **The "Never-Level" Earthquake:**
   - He slams the ground, causing the environment to tilt. This makes it impossible for the heroes to find a flat surface to "Freeze" their powers.

### 📖 Story Role: The Natural Enemy

Kobu is the ultimate test for our heroes. Kuri-0’s high-speed spins and Sofuto’s airy lightness mean nothing against a target that is **Solid, Uneven Ice**.

In the comic, Kuri-0 once tried to spin through Kobu without checking his "level," and his signature blade shattered into a thousand pieces. Now, Kuri-0 must find a way to "flatten" Kobu’s defenses before he can even start the fight.

> **Character Quote:** *"Spin all you want, little Ninja. My surface is jagged, my heart is solid, and your blades are... fragile. Welcome to the Deep Freeze!"*

### 🧪 The Science in the Script: The "Leveling" Law

Kobu represents the real-world danger of the Ninja Creami:

- **The Expansion Trap:** Kobu explains that when water freezes without enough sugar (**PAC**), it expands upward. This "Hump" is a concentrated point of stress.
- **Mechanical Failure:** In the comic, when Kobu appears, the "Warning Light" on Kuri-0’s visor blinks red. This is a nod to the "Install" light on the machine—if you aren't level, you aren't safe.
- **The Solution:** To defeat Kobu, the heroes have to use **Heat-Jitsu** (warm water or a scraper) to level him out before the final spin.
