# 🥷 Character File: Sofuto Sābu (Soft Swirl)

While Kuri-0 focuses on the "milling" and density, **Sofuto** is the master of **Volume**. He is a massive, bulky powerhouse who moves with the weightlessness of a cloud. He represents the "Lite Ice Cream" and "Creami Deluxe" functions—turning heavy ingredients into airy miracles.

## 🎨 Visual Design (Anime Style)

- **The Body:** He is a "Big Guy" character—broad shoulders and huge arms—but he doesn't walk; he bounces. His armor is rounded and puffy, resembling a pressurized marshmallow.
- **The Hair:** A massive, white, whipped-style afro that looks like a perfect soft-serve peak.
- **The Gear:** He wears **"Aero-Turbine" Gauntlets**. These huge gloves suck in air and inject it into anything he touches.
- **Color Palette:** Pastel Blue, Creamy White, and "Low-Cal" Mint Green.
- **The Vibe:** He is the "little brother" figure to Kuri-0—over-enthusiastic, loud, and always hungry.

## ⚔️ Combat Style: "The Cloud-Weight Fist"

Sofuto’s fighting style is based on **Overrun** (the percentage of air in ice cream).

1. **The "Lite" Impact:**
   - Sofuto hits an enemy with a massive fist, but instead of crushing them, the impact "fluffs" them, sending them floating helplessly into the air.
2. **Pressure Expansion:**
   - He can expand his armor by injecting air, becoming a giant bouncy shield to protect Kuri-0 from "Crystal Shards."
3. **The Frothy Fog:**
   - He releases a mist of aerated dairy that blinds enemies. It’s like fighting inside a giant whipped cream canister.

## 📖 Story Role: The "Volume" Specialist

In the comic, Kuri-0 often makes things too "dense" and technical. Sofuto is there to remind him that **"Life needs more air!"** While Kuri-0 handles the frozen bricks, Sofuto is the expert on **Protein Fluff**. He can take a tiny amount of liquid and, through sheer "Spin-Jitsu," turn it into a meal that fills an entire bowl.

> **Character Quote:** *"Why be heavy when you can be huge? Watch me turn this pebble into a mountain of foam! Go, Lite Ice Cream Mode!"*

## 🧪 The Science in the Script: The Overrun Gauge

In their world, "The Void" is a desert of dense, icy rocks. Sofuto uses his knowledge of **Emulsifiers** to fix it:

- **The Protein Link:** Sofuto explains to the reader that air needs "anchors." He uses **Casein** and **Whey** (protein) as the "glue" that holds his air bubbles together.
- **The Calorie Paradox:** Because he is so "bulky" but made of air, he is actually the lightest member of the team. He can walk on water or snow without sinking, a literal "Lite" ninja.
