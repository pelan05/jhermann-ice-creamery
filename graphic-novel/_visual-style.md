## Visual Style: Anime-inspired, but with a big cyberpunk influence

- Features highly stylized character art with large, expressive eyes and dramatic lighting.
- Uses vibrant, bold, cel-shaded colors, and heavy inking.
- Highly detailed muscular anatomy
- Futuristic, sleek objects with glowing accents.
- Dynamic "camera" angles and speed lines create energy.
- Cinematic highlights, dramatic "low-angle" camera perspective.
- Theming: Action, Superheroes, Epic Fantasy.
