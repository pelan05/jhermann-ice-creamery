# ⚒️ The Blade-Smith of the "Frozen Core": Master Resu

Deep within the sub-zero caves of the **Dairy Alps** lives **Master Resu**, the only blacksmith capable of forging weapons that don't shatter in the absolute zero of the Crystal Clan’s domain.

## The Forging of the X-Processor
Master Resu didn't use heat to forge Kuri-0's blade; he used **Cryo-Pressure**.
* **The Steel:** He used "Hardened Chrome-Alloy," a metal that actually gets tougher the colder it gets.
* **The Balance:** To ensure the 4-blade star was perfectly balanced, he had to forge it inside a rotating centrifuge. Even a 0.1mg weight difference would cause the blade to wobble and explode during a **High-RPM Spin**.

## 📖 The "Blade-Breaking" Prophecy
Master Resu warns every ninja who visits his forge of the **"Three Curses of the Machine"**:

1.  **The Curse of the Loose Nut:** If the central hub isn't tightened by the "Spindle-Key," the blade will fly off and destroy the ninja's own armor.
2.  **The Curse of the Liquid Core:** If you spin before the 24-hour mark, the center of the enemy will remain liquid, causing a "Splash-Back" that ruins your stealth.
3.  **The Curse of the Over-Fill:** If you try to process too much at once, the pressure will rise until the vault door itself blows off its hinges.
