# Stunning Hazelnut Gelato [24oz]

> From the transcript of [Stunning Hazelnut Gelato in the Ninja Creami - ULTRA smooth](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MRJ7khMUhdk).

**Composition of the base**

| Fat % | Sugar % | Total Solids % | Overrun |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| ≈14.9% | ≈23.1% | ≈44.9% | ≈0-5% |

Looking for a super smooth hazelnut flavor without any of the grit? This recipe skips home-ground nuts in the base and uses a high-quality hazelnut extract instead to keep things silky. The flavor is taken to the next level with a homemade hazelnut praline swirl. One thing to watch out for: your praline might "split" (the fat separates from the solids) while blending. Don't worry! You can easily fix this by adding a tiny bit of warm water mixed with lecithin to bring it back together into a smooth paste.

## Ingredients

**Gelato Base**

*   567g (≈2.4 cups) **[Cream 18% / Half and Half](https://afcd.foodstandards.gov.au/fooddetails.aspx?PFKID=F003267)**
*   46g (≈0.5 cups) **[Skim Milk Powder](https://afcd.foodstandards.gov.au/fooddetails.aspx?PFKID=F005652)**
*   124g (≈0.6 cups) **[White Sugar](https://afcd.foodstandards.gov.au/fooddetails.aspx?PFKID=F008976)**
*   4g (≈1.3 tsp) Guar Gum (Optional stabilizer)
*   3g (≈0.6 tsp) **[Soy Lecithin](https://afcd.foodstandards.gov.au/fooddetails.aspx?PFKID=F005158)**
*   5g (≈1 tsp) Hazelnut Extract (LorAnn recommended)

*Subtotal Weight*: 749g

**Hazelnut Praline (Mix-in)**

*   100g (≈0.7 cups) **[Hazelnuts, roasted](https://afcd.foodstandards.gov.au/fooddetails.aspx?PFKID=F006098)**
*   100g (≈0.5 cups) **[White Sugar](https://afcd.foodstandards.gov.au/fooddetails.aspx?PFKID=F008976)**

*Subtotal Weight*: 200g

## Directions

1.  **Base Prep**: In a large bowl, whisk the cream, skim milk powder, sugar, guar gum, and lecithin until the mixture is smooth and lumpy bits are gone.
2.  **Flavor**: Stir in the hazelnut extract.
3.  **Aging**: Pour the mix into a Ninja Creami Deluxe tub and freeze for 24 hours until rock solid.
4.  **Praline Swirl**: Melt 100g of sugar in a pan until it turns into a dark golden caramel. Pour it over the roasted hazelnuts and let it cool completely at room temperature. Once hard, blend it into a semi-smooth paste (this takes ≈5 minutes).
5.  **Churning**: Process the frozen tub on the "Gelato" setting. 
6.  **Hardening**: Fold in the praline as a mix-in. If the gelato feels too soft, pop it back in the freezer for about an hour to firm up.
