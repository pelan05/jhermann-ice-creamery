# DIRECTIONS

 1. **Mix cornstarch slurry**: In a small bowl, whisk the cornstarch with a few tablespoons of the milk until smooth.
 1. **Heat base**: In a saucepan, whisk the dry ingredients into the remaining milk. Bring to a gentle boil over medium heat.
 1. **Thicken**: Stir in the cornstarch slurry and simmer for 1‚Äì2 minutes until slightly thickened.
 1. **Add cream cheese and VG**: Remove from heat, add VG, and whisk in the softened cream cheese until fully incorporated.
 1. **Chill**: Cool the mixture quickly (ice bath or fridge) until completely cold ‚Äî ideally overnight.
 1. Add remaining ingredients (to the MAX line) and stir with a spoon.
 1. For better results, let the base age in the fridge (covered, lid on), for a few hours or over night. This helps flavor development and gum hydration, especially with unheated bases.
 1. Freeze for 24h with lid on, then spin as usual. Flatten any humps before that.
 1. Process with RE-SPIN mode when not creamy enough after the first spin.
