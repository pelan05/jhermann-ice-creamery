# Leite Creme (Deluxe)

Portuguese *leite creme* translated into a low-sugar Creami base:
vanilla custard, lemon peel, cinnamon, and a light burnt-sugar caramel note.

This sample follows the repo's usual logic: keep real sugar low, then rebuild
softness and body with sweetener blending, glycerin, protein-rich dairy,
inulin, and a little stabilizer.

Process on *Light Ice Cream*, do a scrape-down, and finish with a respin.
Add a little extra caramel syrup on top if you want the classic brûléed finish.

# INGREDIENTS

ℹ️ Brand names are in square brackets `[...]`.

**Prep**

  - _250ml_ [Soy milk 1.6% (sugar-free) \[Berief\]](/ice-creamery/info/ingredients/#soy-milk){target="_blank"}<sup>↗</sup>
  - _1 stick_ Cinnamon • simmer briefly in the milk
  - _2 strips_ Lemon peel • no white pith

**Wet**

  - _250g_ [Topfen / Quark 0.6% \[Berchtesgadener\]](/ice-creamery/info/ingredients/#quark-topfen){target="_blank"}<sup>↗</sup> • *alternative:* low-fat cream cheese
  - _150ml_ [Soy milk 1.6% (sugar-free) \[Berief\]](/ice-creamery/info/ingredients/#soy-milk){target="_blank"}<sup>↗</sup>
  - _20g_ [Glycerin (E422, VG) \[hd-line\]](/ice-creamery/info/ingredients/#vegetable-glycerin-glycerol-vg-e422){target="_blank"}<sup>↗</sup> • POD = 60%; GI = 5; Density = 1.26 g/ml
  - _30ml_ Syrup Caramel (low-sugar) [Sukrin] • for the custard / brûléed sugar note
  - _6ml_ Vanilla Extract (w/ alcohol) [Native Vanilla]

**Dry**

  - _40g_ [SweEX (Erythritol + Xylitol 3:2)](/ice-creamery/info/ingredients/#sweex-erythritol-xylitol-blend){target="_blank"}<sup>↗</sup> • *alternative:* 53g allulose or dextrose
  - _15g_ [Inulin \[Vit4ever\]](/ice-creamery/info/ingredients/#inulin){target="_blank"}<sup>↗</sup> • Sweetness = 8%; GI ~= 0
  - _10g_ [Whey + Casein protein (grass-fed) \[Vilgain\]](/ice-creamery/info/ingredients/#whey-protein){target="_blank"}<sup>↗</sup>
  - _5g_ [Waxy Maize Starch (E1442) \[Ultratex\]](/ice-creamery/info/ingredients/#waxy-maize-starch-e1442){target="_blank"}<sup>↗</sup>
  - _5g_ [Salty Stability \[Inulin / GMS / CMC / Guar / XG / Salt\]](/ice-creamery/S/Salty%20Stability/){target="_blank"}<sup>↗</sup>
  - _1 pinch_ Ground cinnamon

**Fill to MAX**

  - _≈40ml_ [Soy milk 1.6% (sugar-free) \[Berief\]](/ice-creamery/info/ingredients/#soy-milk){target="_blank"}<sup>↗</sup>
  - _≈2 drops_ Flavor drops Vanilla (sucralose) [IronMaxx] • to taste
  - _≈2 drops_ Flavor drops Caramel (sucralose) [IronMaxx] • to taste

**Topping Options**

  - _10ml_ Syrup Caramel (low-sugar) [Sukrin]
  - _1 pinch_ Ground cinnamon

# DIRECTIONS

 1. Heat the milk from the "prep" section with the cinnamon stick and lemon peel until hot, but not boiling. Let steep for 10 min, then remove the aromatics.
 1. Add the infused milk and the other wet ingredients to an empty Creami tub.
 1. Weigh and mix dry ingredients, easiest by adding them to a jar with a secure lid and shaking vigorously.
 1. Pour into the tub and *QUICKLY* use an immersion blender on full speed to homogenize everything.
 1. Let the blender run until thickeners are properly hydrated, up to 1-2 min. Or blend again after waiting that time.
 1. Add remaining ingredients (to the MAX line) and stir with a spoon.
 1. For better results, let the base age in the fridge (covered, lid on) for a few hours or over night.
 1. Freeze for 24h with lid on, then spin as usual. Flatten any humps before that.
 1. Process with RE-SPIN mode when not creamy enough after the first spin.
 1. Serve with a little caramel syrup and cinnamon if you want a more obvious *leite creme* finish.
