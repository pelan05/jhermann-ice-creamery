# Low-Sugar Recipe Logic

This repo treats sugar as a functional ingredient, not just a sweetener.

- Keep sweetness, softness, and body as separate design goals.
- Lower sucrose hardens the base, so replace its freezing-point effect with a blend such as `SweEX`, glycerin, a little alcohol, lactose, and sometimes low-sugar syrups.
- Replace sugar's bulk and creaminess with protein-rich dairy, inulin, modified starch, and small amounts of gums or premixes such as `ICSv2` / `Salty Stability`.
- Build flavor with strong ingredients that do not add much sugar: vanilla, cocoa, coffee, spices, citrus zest, extracts, and flavor drops.
- Use fruit, dates, syrups, or candy sparingly and deliberately, mainly for character.
- Keep the base scientifically balanced instead of "healthy" in name only: a Creami-friendly target is usually PAC / FPDF around `28..32`, then adjust after testing.
- Finalize recipes in the spreadsheet, where macros, PAC / FPDF, MSNF, and serving values can be checked before exporting to Markdown.
